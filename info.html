Jamie Kronick is a fine art and commercial photographer in Ottawa, Canada.

His work, predominantly rooted in portraiture, strikes a balance between people, place and time. Combining his love of music and the arts with his need to document the relationship of his subjects and the environment, his work brings you a glimpse into the lives of others.
